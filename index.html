About The Event

The HackHoodie presents a 48-hour hackathon, in association with Major League Hacking (MLH) for the first time. This hackathon is open to every inquisitive individual around the world. Get ready for a hackathon that focusses on learning made fun. The HackHoodie provides you with the opportunity and an environment to assimilate the basics and hands-on experience on different technologies. It will be a great chance to interact with diverse minds around the globe. There will be giveaways and swags for the winners as well as the participants. See you there.
Happy Hacking!

F.A.Q

  • What is a Hackathon?

    A hackathon is an invention marathon where you can work with people around the globe to build a project related to technology! It isn't about hacking into a system, it's instead about hacking something together and learning a great deal in the process.

  • Who can participate?

    Everyone is welcome to participate, be it, students, professionals or aliens from different planets!

  • How long will it be on for?

    The event will be running for about 48 hours from 26th March to 27th March.

  • How much does it cost?

    Nothing. Participation to HackHoodie is completely free, thanks to our sponsors!

  • Where can I find updates?

    We have an active Discord for communication, along with social media handles

  • How big can a team be?

    You can form teams of 2 to 4 people. Most teams aim to have a mix of people with both design and developer skills. Remember we won't accept project submissions from individual applicants
